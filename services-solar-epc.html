Solar EPC Delivery Models

CAPEX Model

  • Investment: Upfront customer investment
  • Ownership: Customer owns the system
  • Services: Turnkey EPC services
  • Maintenance: After-sales support

OPEX Model

  • Investment: Starlink investment
  • Ownership: Starlink owns the system
  • Payment: Pay for electricity consumed
  • Maintenance: Hassle-free maintenance
